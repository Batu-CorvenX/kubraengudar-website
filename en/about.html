About Me

Psychologist Kübra Engüdar

Clinical Psychologist · Family Counselor

After graduating from Uludağ University's Psychology Department, she completed her master's degree in Clinical Psychology at Arel University, earning the title of Clinical Psychologist.

She completed the Family Counseling Certificate Program at Biruni University, also earning the qualification of Family Counselor.

She has gained experience working with children, adolescents, adults, and families in various clinical and educational settings, and has contributed academically as a guest lecturer at university. She currently lives in New York and continues to offer online counseling.
