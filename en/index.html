About Me

Psychologist Kübra Engüdar

After graduating with honors from Uludağ University's Psychology Department, she completed her master's degree in Clinical Psychology at Istanbul Arel University with high honors, earning the title of Clinical Psychologist.

In 2023, she completed the Family Counseling Certificate Program at Biruni University with 450 hours of training and supervision, earning the qualification of Family Counselor.

Since 2025, she has been living in New York and continues to offer online counseling. She is a member of the American Psychological Association (APA), the Turkish Psychological Association, and the EMDR Turkey Association.

Process

How does counseling work?

01

Initial consultation

We get to know each other through a brief free consultation. I listen to your needs and we evaluate together whether the process is right for you.

02

Personalized plan

In the first meetings, we make sense of your situation together. We create a personalized working plan tailored to your goals.

03

Transformation

Through regular meetings, you begin to know yourself better and start coping with challenges in a healthier way.
